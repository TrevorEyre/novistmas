What is Novistmas?

Novistmas is a fun new holiday on the first Saturday of November. On Novistmas we celebrate with traditions such as singing Novistmas Carols and having ugly sweater contests with family. Novistmas is fun because we can spend time with family. It also gives us another holiday to get excited about between Halloween and Christmas.

Foods Traditions

Our food traditions are men cooking instead of women, drinking eggnog, and eating rice pudding (that the men cook)

While girls have done all the cooking the whole year, men(for the first time ever) are replacing the women's role. From the whatever is for dinner to the rice pudding for desert is cooked by men. We also drink lots of eggnog. Personally, Novistmas is the only holiday I have ever had eggnog with.
